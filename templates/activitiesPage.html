NURSERY ACTIVITIES

Quality education in the early years of Preschool sets a firm foundation in the over-all development of a child.

“Every child is gifted, they just unwrap their packages at different times.”

In our school, the nursery curriculum provides a right array of activities to help children move towards achieving their early learning goals. Each child is unique and to unearth that talent various activities are conducted by the vibrant, innovative teachers of the Nursery section.

INTER-HOUSE ACTIVITIES

In Arya Public School, we not only focus on academic excellence but also character & overall growth of the students.

The school is divided into four houses:

  • Dayanand
  • Vivekanand
  • Shradhanand
  • Virjanand

Regular interhouse competitions in recitation, drawing, painting debates etc. are held. The idea is to inculcate a sense of healthy competition among students.

SPORTS ACTIVITY

“A healthy mind and a healthy body go hand in hand.”

Sports encompass more than just the benefit of physical activity; it increases the self-esteem and mental alertness of every participant, whatever be the sport one involves in.

The Widest possible range of sports activities in the school includes, taekwondo, kho-kho, badminton and athletics. ARYA PUBLIC SCHOOL lays great emphasis on sports activities for the complete development of the students’ personality.
